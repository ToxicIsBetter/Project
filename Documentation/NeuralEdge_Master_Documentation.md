## 1. The Core Engine: Dual-Head Transformer (d32/h4/l2)

The intelligence of NeuralEdge is powered by a custom-built transformer architecture, fine-tuned specifically for the volatile cryptocurrency markets.

### **1.1. Architecture Specification**
*   **Blueprint Name:** NeuralEdge Challenger (GS Run 82 Winner).
*   **d32 (Embedding Dimension):** The model processes 32 parallel data features. This leaner width was chosen over larger models (d64/d128) because it significantly improved generalization and reduced overfitting on noisy Bitcoin data.
*   **h4 (Attention Heads):** The model uses 4 parallel attention heads to cross-reference short-term price spikes, long-term on-chain trends, retail sentiment, and whale movements simultaneously.
*   **l2 (Layers):** 2 stacked Transformer Encoder layers provide the depth necessary to understand complex market regimes (e.g., "Whale accumulation during retail panic").

### **1.2. Accuracy & Performance**
*   **Verified Test Accuracy:** **63.96%**.
*   **F1-Score:** **0.6209** (Balancing precision and recall to minimize false signals).
*   **Inference Threshold:** **0.65**. The model requires a 65% probability to issue a signal, ensuring only high-conviction trades are executed.
*   **Inference Speed:** Average latency is **14ms**.

## 2. Page 1: The Terminal (Operational Command)

The Terminal is the primary dashboard for real-time monitoring and signal execution.

### **2.1. Mission Control Metrics**
*   **BTC Price:** Current exchange price with a 24h percentage change indicator. 🟢 (Green) = Gain, 🔴 (Red) = Loss.
*   **Tomorrow's Signal:** 
    *   **ACCUMULATE (Green):** Model predicts an upward move.
    *   **DISTRIBUTE (Red):** Model predicts a downward move.
    *   **HOLD (Blue):** Confidence is too low to trade; capital preservation mode.
*   **Confidence (%):** How far the probability is from the 0.65 threshold.
*   **Risk Level:**
    *   **LOW:** Confidence > 70%.
    *   **MEDIUM:** Confidence 55% - 70%.
    *   **HIGH:** Confidence < 55% (Triggers an automatic 'HOLD').

### **2.2. Interactive Charting**
*   **Historical Line:** 1-year view of actual BTC price movements.
*   **Prediction Line (Dashed):** Visual extension from the latest price point to "Tomorrow." The line drifts UP or DOWN to reflect the current AI signal.
*   **Hover Crosshair:** Interactive tool showing the exact date and price at any point in history.

### **2.3. Neural Log**
A real-time feed that visualizes the "Forward Pass" of the model. It logs the exact timestamp and model confidence every time a new data point is processed.

## 3. Page 2: The Engine Room (Technical Deep-Dive)

The Engine Room visualizes the internal "Expert Logic" of the two model heads.

### **3.1. Head 1: On-Chain & Fundamentals (The Whales)**
*   **AdrActCnt (Active Wallets):** A count of unique addresses interacting with the blockchain. Measures adoption and usage.
*   **CapMVRVCur (MVRV Z-Score):** Measures if Bitcoin is over or undervalued relative to what everyone actually paid for their coins.
*   **FlowInExUSD (Whale Flows):** Large-scale Bitcoin movements into exchanges. High inflows usually signal institutional selling pressure.

### **3.2. Head 2: Sentiment Analysis (The Psychology)**
*   **Fear & Greed Index:** A metric ranging from 0 (Extreme Fear - Buying Opportunity) to 100 (Extreme Greed - Distribution Signal).
*   **Google Trends:** Quantifies retail hype by tracking search volume for "Bitcoin," "Scam," and "Buy Crypto."
*   **Social Velocity:** Measures how fast the conversation about Bitcoin is accelerating. A high velocity usually precedes high price volatility.

### **3.3. The Fusion Layer**
Visualizes the 3-step synchronization process:
1.  **SIGNAL AGGREGATION:** Merging the two heads into a master data vector.
2.  **RISK WEIGHTING:** The transformer weights which head is more reliable in the current market.
3.  **FINAL INFERENCE:** Mapping the result to a trade command.

## 4. Page 3: The Simulator (Transparency & Backtesting)

The Simulator provides the historical proof of the model's accuracy.

### **4.1. Success Summary**
*   **Cumulative Growth (%):** Total profit generated by following model signals since inception. 
*   **Success Rate (%):** Percentage of trades that resulted in a positive gain the following day.

### **4.2. The Transparency Table**
Every trade is recorded with full accountability.
*   **Date:** When the prediction was made.
*   **Signal:** The model's recommendation (ACCUMULATE/DISTRIBUTE).
*   **Confidence:** The certainty level of that specific prediction.
*   **Price:** The BTC price at the time of the prediction.
*   **Next Day Return:** The actual price movement that occurred after the prediction.
*   **Invested Capital Growth:** Tracking the growth of a $100,000 starting account.

### **4.3. Risk Analysis Metrics**
*   **Sharpe Ratio:** Measures your "Efficiency." Tells you how much return you get for every unit of risk taken. (High = Professional, Low = Lucky).
*   **Max Drawdown:** The "Pain Metric." Shows the largest peak-to-trough drop in the account value, identifying worst-case scenario losses.

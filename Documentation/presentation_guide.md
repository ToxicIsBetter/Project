**Goal:** Hook the audience conceptually, clearly define the NeuralEdge product, and fluidly cover every single feature on the platform.

---

*   **Action:** Start on the Home Page (`index.html`).
*   **Narrative:** 
"Have you ever stared at a crypto chart, trying to separate the real signal from the noise? We all know Bitcoin is driven by complex, overlapping variables that no human brain can track simultaneously. 

Enter **NeuralEdge**. Think of it as your institutional-grade AI co-pilot. It’s a quantitative trading platform that doesn't just track current prices—it actually *predicts* market direction using a cutting-edge Dual-Head Transformer model.

When you land on our home page, notice the unified **Global Navigation Bar** anchoring the experience, complete with a glowing logo and a pulse indicator confirming our 'Inference Engine' is live. Right below, our **Live News Ticker** constantly scrolls macro and on-chain intelligence, seated just above our professional **Risk Disclaimer Banner**.

Behind the pulsing 'Inference Live' badge, an **animated SVG background** mimics the very neural nodes powering our logic. And before you even click 'Enter', those four **Responsive Metrics Preview Cards** at the bottom instantly give you the bottom line—Live Price, AI Recommendation, Confidence, and Latency."

## 🕒 Minute 2: The Terminal (1:00 - 2:00)

"But let's put you in the driver's seat. Welcome to the Neural Terminal—your trading mission control.

Now, draw your eyes to the **Perpetual Signal Chart**. As you hover your cursor, the interactive **crosshair and tooltip** spring to life, overlaying today's price trajectory with the AI’s actual prediction nodes.

Just below that, the scrolling **Neural Inference Log** gives you complete, transparent insight into every micro-movement and calculation the system makes."

## 🕒 Minute 3: The Simulator (2:00 - 3:00)

*   **Action:** Navigate to the Simulation page (`simulator.html`) and run a date range.
*   **Narrative:** 
"Of course, you shouldn’t trust any AI blindly. That’s why we built the Challenger Simulator—a no-compromise backtesting engine loaded with *real* historical inference data.

You simply dial in your **Starting Capital**, pick your dates from the interactive **Calendar Inputs**, and hit **'Run Engine Simulation'**.

It catalogs every single historical trade, compares the forecast against the absolute truth of the next day, and automatically grades it as 'Accurate' or a 'Deviation'."
